10 Best Multipurpose WordPress Themes for 2020

Multipurpose themes are the perfect solution for such instances. When using a multipurpose theme, you do not have to worry if it will look good on your site because these sites are designed to serve multiple niches all at the same time.

As the name suggests, these themes are packed with various layouts that go well with almost all topics. Whether it is fashion, charity, travel, or a corporate purpose, these themes blend in with any niche to make the site look appealing.

1. Divi

Divi is one of the most fabulous WordPress themes that will forever change the way you build a website. Ever since its release, it has attracted a lot of attention and has continued to be the most discussed and most used theme in the WordPress market. This incredible theme has been acclaimed over and over again for its refined blending of charming looks and resilient features.

This multipurpose theme can be used on any site without a second thought. With its immensely long list of features and functions, you can use your most creative side and design a delightful site packed with the latest, trendiest features. From displaying multiple fancy posts to editing elements, you can go on and on until you are satisfied with how your site appears. Once you know you are ready for your clients, you can go live publishing it on social media and other platforms.

2. H-Code

H-Code is a powerful multipurpose WordPress theme with tons of amazing features. This theme offers you over 57 ready to be used demo themes and over 190 demo pages, so the design options are almost unlimited. As you get the premium Visual Composer drag and drop plugin for free, you don't have to stick to the preset designs because you can easily create a custom design without writing a single line of code.

H-Code is fully responsive and retina ready so that your site looks great on all kinds of devices. The theme is created on Twitter Bootstrap and coded with HTML5.You can use H-Code theme to create an online shop as it is fully compatible with WooCommerce plugin. The theme offers you beautiful, modern, ready-to-use shop designs.

3. Salient

Salient is a powerful WordPress multipurpose theme that you can use for anything from a business site to a simple blog. The design possibilities are unlimited with the Salient theme, and you can easily create the perfect design to start your online venture.Salient is one of the top-selling creative WordPress designs that have a strong team to make sure that everything goes smoothly with your site so you can concentrate on growing your business.

The theme is fully responsive and retina ready, and it will make sure that your site looks great on all kinds of modern devices. It comes with a powerful theme options panel where you can manage all the details related to your site and set up everything you need. Salient offers you very easy customization possibilities; you can design your entire site with the drag and drop website builder.

4. Brooklyn

Brooklyn is a superb one-page parallax theme that can be used for any kind of site. With Brooklyn, you can get a most professional and trendy look that will make your site look incredible. With sixteen premade demos, a powerful admin panel, and easy customization options, you will have great control over your site's looks. You can edit each element to match the latest trends.

It also allows you to manage a classy portfolio to impress your clients. The theme has a table manager that allows the creation of tables in seconds.Brooklyn is also easily translatable and comes at a very affordable rate. Best of all, you can skip all the coding and complete your development in a matter of minutes.

5. Enfold

Enfold is a great theme that can be used to create a site within any niche. With tons of high-tech features, great support, and superb demo content, this is a marvelous multi-purpose theme. It is a user-friendly and intuitive theme that can help you create your site without any coding.

It is adaptable to all browsers and is compatible with the latest WordPress versions. The theme is WooCommerce and WPML ready so that you can sell your products to a wider customer base.

6. Stockholm

The Stockholm WordPress theme is another fancy theme that allows you to build a website instantly and market your business in style. It comes with 40 stylish and fully equipped demo layouts, seven pre-defined header styles, unlimited Google fonts, and five-star support.

The theme is highly customizable, and you can easily combine the features and elements from its demos and customize it. This high resolution, widget-ready theme is an Elite Author product, so you know you can count on it. Users of this theme express great satisfaction with its design quality, customizability, and flexibility.

7. Uplift

Uplift is a stunning multipurpose theme that comes with great visual aesthetics. You can use this theme for any kind of website: a personal blog, a fashion blog, a magazine blog, or any other kind of blog.

The strong drag and drop builder contains fifty-four elements that can be used to create a stunning website. You also have nine beautiful demo layouts, three shop styles, forty-two pre-built pages, and a lot more. This theme is WooCommerce enabled and has been optimized for SEO.

8. Impreza

The next theme that you should check out is the Impreza. This is a wonderful combination of visual appeal and robust functionality. With this theme, you can create a successful blog design in less than 10 minutes, and that's without having to code a single line.

You also get a fantastic slider revolution, visual composer, various plugin integration, and a lot more.The one-click demo import makes it even easier for you to design your page because you just need to drag your elements and drop them wherever you want them to be on the site.

9. Mega WP Theme

Mega is another fantastic multipurpose WordPress theme that comes loaded with various layout options: more than 29 elements, a theme customizer, and a lot more. This theme is compatible with the latest versions of WordPress and works wonderfully with tons of robust plugins to make your site run smoothly.

10. Monaco

Monaco is a versatile, super responsive, SEO friendly WordPress theme that fits all sites with its multi-concept designs. This theme is a perfect combination of elegance and sharp features.

It also comes with some amazing built-in premium plugins like the Visual Composer, Drag and Drop Website Builder, Revolution Slider, WooCommerce integration, and more. Although you are purchasing just a single theme, you get many other brilliant functions to use on your site for free.
